Privacy Policy

CleanPhoneLauncher · Last updated: 28 June 2026

CleanPhoneLauncher (the “app”) is a home-screen launcher for Android, developed by RikoAppDev. This policy explains what data the app handles. In short: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app uses stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. The app has no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. It does not require an account and does not connect to the internet to send your information anywhere.

Data stored on your device

To provide its features, the app stores the following locally and only on your device:

  • Favorite apps — the list of apps you mark as favorites (stored in a local database).
  • Clock preference — your selected clock style (stored in local app settings).

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

Permissions and why they are used

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — to show the list of installed apps in the launcher drawer. This is a core function of a launcher.
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS — optional; only to display your recently used apps. You grant it manually and can revoke it any time in system settings. Usage data is read on-device and never transmitted.
  • REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES — to open the system uninstall dialog when you choose to uninstall an app.
  • SET_ALARM — to open the clock/alarm app when you tap the clock.

Sharing

The app does not share any data with the developer or any third party, because it does not collect any.

Children

The app does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this same URL with a new “Last updated” date.
